# SEO Rubric — 100 Points (Quick Evaluation)

**Target:** ≥80/100 before publishing

## How to Score

Evaluate each category 0–10; total is sum (10 categories).

1. **Content & Real Value** — depth, specificity, usefulness
2. **User Intent Match** — answer in first 2-3 paragraphs
3. **E-E-A-T** — sources, expertise, clarity, disclaimers when needed
4. **Readability & Structure** — H2/H3, short paragraphs, lists
5. **Engagement** — useful hook, concrete scenarios, local examples
6. **Link Strategy** — 1-2 internal relevant, 1-2 credible sources; moderate affiliate
7. **Balanced Conversion** — discreet CTA, useful resources; no pressure
8. **Mobile UX** — optimized images, spacing, no "screaming" visuals
9. **Search Intent** — clear alignment (informational vs transactional)
10. **Technical SEO** — alt text, FAQ schema, loading, coherent title/meta

## Score Scale per Category

- **0-2:** weak (contradicts intent / spam / missing sources)
- **3-5:** insufficient (generic, misaligned, serious UX problems)
- **6-7:** acceptable (useful but thin / missing sources or links)
- **8-9:** very good (aligned, clear, minor gaps)
- **10:** excellent (perfectly meets criterion)

## Anti-Penalty Guardrails (Google 2024+)

- ❌ Avoid "affiliate-heavy": max 2 (exceptional 3) links; only in dedicated boxes
- ❌ No manipulative language or unverifiable promises
- ❌ Avoid gradient-spam, emoji-spam, repetitive CTA; keep design sober
- ✅ Add credible sources (WHO, reference works, Wikipedia-RO)
- ✅ Maintain informational intent; don't transform article into sales funnel

## Quick-Fix (under 5 minutes)

1. Cut link-spam; move affiliate to "Resources" box
2. Add 1 credible source + 1 internal link
3. Rewrite intro with clear answer and context
4. Add 2-4 FAQ questions with schema
5. Optimize alt text and compress large images

## Critical Finding (Nov 2, 2025)

**SEO Balance Discovery:**
- 15+ affiliate links per article → Google penalty
- "PROFIT MASIV" language → trust destruction
- Revenue tables & business spam → bounce rate spike
- Gradient boxes everywhere → mobile UX disaster

**New Standard:**
- 1-2 contextual affiliate links per article
- Professional, informational tone
- User-first content (helpful, not sales)
- Clean design, subtle monetization
- **Estimated SEO score: 80-85/100** (vs 24/100 aggressive)
