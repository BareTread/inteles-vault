## Recent Major Learnings

### ‚úÖ SEO Balance Discovery (Nov 2, 2025)
**Critical Insight:** Aggressive monetization = SEO suicide!

**What We Learned:**
- 15+ affiliate links per article ‚Üí Google penalty
- "PROFIT MASIV" language ‚Üí trust destruction
- Revenue tables & business spam ‚Üí bounce rate spike
- Gradient boxes everywhere ‚Üí mobile UX disaster

**New Standard: Balanced Approach**
- 1-2 contextual affiliate links per article
- Professional, informational tone
- User-first content (helpful, not sales)
- Clean design, subtle monetization
- Estimated SEO score: 80-85/100 (vs 24/100 aggressive)

**See:** [[02-CONTENT-STRATEGY#SEO-Balance]] for full details

## Core Principles

### Content Quality
1. **User-first** - Answer questions genuinely
2. **Evidence-based** - Reference credible sources
3. **Professional** - No manipulation or spam
4. **Mobile-optimized** - 97.5% mobile traffic
5. **Romanian cultural context** - Speak to diaspora

### Monetization Balance
1. **1-2 affiliate links max** per article
2. **Contextual placement** - natural recommendations
3. **Helpful resources** - not aggressive sales
4. **Trust-building** - sustainable long-term revenue
5. **ANPC compliant** - Romanian legal requirements

### SEO Strategy
1. **Comprehensive content** - 2,000+ words
2. **Natural keywords** - no stuffing
3. **FAQ schema** - 6 questions per article
4. **Clean link profile** - quality over quantity
5. **E-E-A-T signals** - credibility markers
